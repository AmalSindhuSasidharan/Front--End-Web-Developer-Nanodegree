Cheetah

Fastest animal in the World
  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Speed: 110 – 120 km/h
  • Mass: 21 – 72 kg
  • Height: 66-94 cm

The cheetah is a large felid of the subfamily Felinae that occurs mainly in eastern and southern Africa and a few parts of Iran.The cheetah is characterised by a slender body, deep chest, spotted coat, a small rounded head, black tear-like streaks on the face, long thin legs and a long spotted tail.The cheetah reaches nearly 70 to 90 cm (28 to 35 in) at the shoulder, and weighs 21–72 kg (46–159 lb).
